FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • How reliable is the test and how accurate is the diagnostics?

    The test can show the users the major characteristics for the current mental health state but the final diagnosis will be given by the therapist.

  • Is this a certified service?

    If PsyScan is available in your country, it means we obtained the medical license there

  • How safely the sensitive data is stored?

    Our technology is being updated constantly to operate more efficiently and the our data storage system guarantees the security of patients’ personal data.

  • How much does the service cost?

    The patients get a free scan via app (IOs & Android) and then the costs will be charged upon using the service of a partner (therapist/clinic), the rates vary per partner For the B2B solution pricing, please reach out to info@psyscan.io to get a quote.
